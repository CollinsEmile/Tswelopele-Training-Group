About Us

Tshwelople Training Group

Our Mission Statement and Quality Policy articulate our commitment to our learners. Learner expectations must be determined, understood, converted into requirements and must have processes designed to exceed them in order to fulfill this Mission and Quality Policy, on an everyday basis. Staying close to our learners is our primary method of determining and understanding their requirements and expectations, and we accomplish this objective through a multitude of channels. These communications and interactions yield clear, explicit requirements and expectations. Our QMS ensures that these requirements are fulfilled with the aim of exceeding learner expectations.

We are the Tswelopele Training Group

Our Goals and Vision

To assist our learners, achieve their utmost potential in quality education, training and development. Work hand in hand with out learners and clients to better their trust and loyalty.
Maintain beneficial partnership and ensure our needs and expectations for training products and services. Keep an excellent and participatory work ethics, rely on the excellence of our stuff and provide a training environment in which they can excel.

Our Mission

We intend to meet and deliver on our learner expectations by constantly improving and updating our skills and resources for Education, Training and Development.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Human dignity
  • Excellent service
  • Exceptional learner experience

Lepi Hoho

Managing Director

Mr. Lepi Hoho, is the Founder of Tswelopele Training Group Graduated with a N Diploma in Electrical/electronics in Engineering at Ekurhuleni Technical College. He ventured in a career in Aviation with the South African Air force in 2008. Within the Aviation industry that is where the passion for training and development started. He started training newly qualified technical members and as the need arose for training, he enrolled for Training and Development courses where he obtained Facilitators, Assessors and Moderators of which he is registerd on ETDP.
He has conducted training successfully in the past 4 years in the Aviation industry (Air force) and is still training technical personnel in the same aviation field till today, he has a passion for training.
In 2020 Tswelopele Training Group was born with the need for training in Engineering, End User Computing, Graphic Designing, Aviation, Health and Safety and all ETDP Training Courses.

Moleboheng Rose Monyaki

Financial Manager

Monyaki Rose Moleboheng a member of Tswelopele Training Group is an agricultural manager and extension officer with more than 7 years experience in community development and research department. Originally from Botshabelo, she completed her Bachelors and Honors Degree in Agricultural Management at the University of the Free State. Before the establishment of Tswelopele Training Group, she did learnership and Internship course at 13A and AgriSETA from 2014-2018 under livestock and Management department. She commenced at agricultural academy as a research assistant from 2018-2020, she supervised all the content development and interpreting research specifications, and develop a work plan that satisfies requirements. She covered admin, receptionist duties as well.

She started facilitating and assessing under International Agricultural Academy for Africa (I3A) in 2019 in partnership with AgriSETA till contract ended. In 2020, Tswelopele Training Group was born with need for training in all ETDP SETA Courses, she is now appointed as a Financial Manager, Facilitator and Assessor (AgriSETA and more) at Tswelopele Training Group.
